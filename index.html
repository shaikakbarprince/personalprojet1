How to make no-bake coffee energy balls

These protein balls are quick and easy to make and perfect if you want to give yourself an early-morning or late-afternoon energy boost. On top of that, studies have shown coffee is rich in powerful antioxidants and can help you live longer.

ingredients

  • 1/4 cup oats
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened shredded coconut, plus 2 tbsp for rolling
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 5 tbsp cold brew coffee concentrate
  • 1/4 cup peanut butter
  • 4 tsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup

preparation

  1. Put the oats, shreddded coconut and banana in a blender for 2-3 minutes (blend until fine).
  2. In a large bowl, add the contents from 1. together with the coffee, peanut butter, cocoa powder, chia seeds and maple syrup.
  3. Stir the mixture in the large bowl until well combined.
  4. Roll the mixutre into well sized golf balls and then roll each ball in the additional coconut.
  5. Place each ball separately onto a tray and let the balls settle in the refridgerator for an hour.
  6. Take the balls and place them into an air-tight container and leave them in the refridgerator. Store the balls in the freezer if you would like to enjoy them for longer.
  7. Enjoy!
